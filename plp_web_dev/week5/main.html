Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can I get fiber internet installed?

Standard installations take 48 hours after survey. Priority business installations available within 24 hours with our RapidDeploy service.

Where can I find Hali Connect hotspots?

Access 1,200+ hotspots via our coverage map. Real-time availability and pricing shown for each location - from boda boda shades to urban plazas.

How does the marketplace work?

Buy/sell goods and services seamlessly: 1) Search product 2) Compare real-time metrics 3) Purchase 4) Auto-connect. Select delivery options from our multiple logistics options

What hotspot speeds can I expect?

Minimum 25Mbps guaranteed at all locations. Priority 5G zones deliver up to 300Mbps for seamless 4K streaming.

Is there fiber redundancy?

Triple-path protected core network with automatic failover to satellite links. 99.99% uptime SLA for business fiber plans.

How secure are public hotspots?

Military-grade encryption with auto VPN activation. Session isolation prevents device cross-talk. Real-time threat monitoring included.

How do hotspot payments work?

Prepay via M-Pesa, cards, or wallet. Auto-topup available. Session pauses when credit runs low - no surprise disconnections.
